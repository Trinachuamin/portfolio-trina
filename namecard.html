Name Card Design

Designed by Trina

Brief Summary: A minimalistic and professional name card design that emphasizes easy readability and organized contact details.

Target Audience: Professionals and clients looking for clear and accessible contact information.

Colour Scheme: Warm neutrals with a focus on taupe and blush tones.

Typography: Elegant serif for the name and sans-serif for contact information.

Theme: Minimalistic, professional, and elegant.

Rationale:

The name card design emphasizes clarity and professionalism. The minimalistic approach with soft, neutral tones provides a sophisticated look. By keeping the layout clean, I ensure that the essential contact information is easy to read, which aligns with the principle of clarity. The consistent use of fonts and colors helps to create a unified and polished look, following the principle of consistency.

Mood Board

The mood board showcases a refined color palette of taupe, blush, and neutral shades, supporting a calm and professional aesthetic.
